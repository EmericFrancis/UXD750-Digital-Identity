What is a credit limit?

A credit limit is the maximum amount you can borrow on a credit card.

How we know this?

As an approved GOV.UK Verify identity provider, we can access your credit records under specific legal conditions to allows us to perform identity verification and credit checking. We are regularly audited to ensure the data is used in compliance with regulations.

Why we need to know this?

We need to access and ask questions based on private data (typically financial history, electoral register or utility company data) that isn't publicly accessible so that people pretending to be you are unlikely to be able to answer the questions correctly. This is how we ensure that the person interacting with the service is who they claim to be to verify their identity digitally.
